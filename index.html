Help Center

How does the AI Smart Import work?

When you check the "AI Smart Import" option and upload a spreadsheet, our parser analyzes the headers to match your column labels (e.g. "CO2", "Carbon Emissions (tons)", "Carbon") with our standard "Carbon Emissions (tCO2e)" attribute. Any columns it cannot map with 90%+ confidence are presented in a Mapping Panel for confirmation.

What validation rules are applied?

Data validation scans submissions for numerical limits, bounds, and empty states. For instance, carbon metrics must not be negative, percentage ratios must be between 0 and 100, and reporting periods must fall in standard ranges. Entries with warnings or omissions can be manually inspected and fixed via the Data Validation interface.

Who do I contact for database discrepancies?

For credentials, user role updates, or server connection logs, submit an IT help desk request or email support@esgplatform.com.
